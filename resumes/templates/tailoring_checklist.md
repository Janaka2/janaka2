# 30-Minute Tailoring Checklist

## 1) Job Extraction (8 minutes)
- Copy the job description into notes.
- Highlight top requirements:
  - Core stack
  - Domain context
  - Ownership/seniority expectation
  - Non-functional themes (performance, reliability, compliance)

## 2) Content Mapping (10 minutes)
- Match each top requirement with one concrete bullet from your achievement bank.
- Reorder experience bullets so the most relevant appear first.
- Keep motivation section in a separate, job-specific file.

## 3) Precision Pass (7 minutes)
- Replace generic verbs with specific actions: designed, migrated, optimized, automated, led.
- Add at least 3 measurable outcomes.
- Ensure chronology and dates are consistent.

## 4) Quality Gate (5 minutes)
- Verify grammar and spacing.
- Confirm role title alignment with target position.
- Export final markdown + PDF with naming convention.

## ATS Safety
- Single-column layout.
- Standard headings (Summary, Skills, Experience, Education).
- Avoid heavy tables/icons in ATS version.
